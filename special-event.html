Special Event Stations - K3A, K3R, K3S

We are operating the special event stations K3A, K3R and K3S from Friday, August 26th through Sunday, September 4th, 2022 to promote innovative, free and open amateur radio software.
During the event we honor the 2022 award recipient David Rowe (VK5DGR) and his Codec 2 software project.
As part of the special event we encourage people to submit nominations for the 2023 Amateur Radio Software Award.

More information along with an operating schedule will be posted here at a closer time.
