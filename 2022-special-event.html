Special Event Stations - K3A, K3R, K3S

We are operating the special event stations K3A, K3R and K3S from Friday, August 26th through Sunday, September 4th, 2022 to promote innovative, free and open amateur radio software.
During the event we honor the 2022 award recipient ?? and his/her ?? software project.
As part of the special event we encourage people to submit nominations for the 2023 Amateur Radio Software Award.

Schedule:

The operating schedule may be updated throughout the week.

Operators are the Amateur Radio Software Award committee members Claus (AE0S) for K3A, Kun (N7DMR) for K3S, and Rich (K0EB) for K3R.

For SSB we'll try to operate in the following frequency ranges:

  • 20m: 14.240 - 14.260 MHz
  • 40m: 7,160 - 7,190 MHz
