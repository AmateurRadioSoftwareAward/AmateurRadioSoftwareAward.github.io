Special Event Stations - K6A, K6R, K6S

We are operating the special event stations K6A, K6R and K6S from Friday, November 7th through Monday, October 17th, 2025 to promote innovative, free and open amateur radio software.
During the event we honor the 2025 award recipients Sebastian Delmont (KI2D) for Ham2K Portable Logger.
As part of the special event we encourage people to submit nominations for the 2026 Amateur Radio Software Award.
