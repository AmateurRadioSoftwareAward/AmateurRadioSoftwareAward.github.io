Special Event Stations - K2A, K2R, K2S

We are operating the special event stations K2A, K2R and K2S from Friday, August 27th through Sunday, September 5th, 2021 to promote innovative, free and open amateur radio software.
During the event we honor the 2021 award recipient Jordan Sherer (KN4CRD) and his JS8Call software project.
As part of the special event we encourage people to submit nominations for the 2022 Amateur Radio Software Award.

Schedule:

The operating schedule may be updated throughout the week.

Operators are the Amateur Radio Software Award committee members Claus (AE0S) for K2A, Kun (N7DMR) for K2S, and Rich (K0EB) for K2R.

For SSB we'll try to operate in the following frequency ranges:

  • 20m: 14.240 - 14.260 MHz
  • 40m: 7,160 - 7,190 MHz
