Special Event Station - K1A

We are operating the special event station K1A from Friday, September 25th through Sunday, October 4th, 2020 to promote innovative, free and open amateur radio software.
During the event we also honor the 2020 award recipient Anthony Good and his K3NG Arduino CW Keyer software project.
As part of the special event we encourage people to submit nominations for the 2021 Amateur Radio Software Award.

Schedule:

The operating schedule is still a work in progress.

We'll try to operate in the following frequency ranges:

  • 20m: 14.240 - 14.260 MHz
  • 40m: 7,160 - 7,190 MHz
