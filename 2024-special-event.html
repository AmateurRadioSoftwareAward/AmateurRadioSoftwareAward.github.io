Special Event Stations - K5A, K5R, K5S

We are operating the special event stations K5A, K5R and K5S from Saturday, September 28th through Sunday, October 6th, 2024 to promote innovative, free and open amateur radio software.
During the event we honor the 2024 award recipients Jakob Ketterl (DD5JFK) for OpenWebRX and Marat Fayzullin (KC1TXE) for OpenWebRX+.
As part of the special event we encourage people to submit nominations for the 2025 Amateur Radio Software Award.

A small encore event for K5S will be over the X-mas week between Tuesday, December 24th through Tuesday, December 31th, 2024 between the holiday festivities. Operations will most likely be on 20m SSB and FT8.

Schedule:

The operating schedule should be taken as a guide where to find the special event station. Based on band condition and other unforseen events operations may change. Most likely some operations will occur in addition to the posted times. Thanks for your understanding.

Operators are the Amateur Radio Software Award committee members Claus (AE0S) for K5A, Kun (N7DMR) for K5S, and Rich (K0EB) for K5R.

Feel free to spot us. We try to spot ourselves when operating SSB. So you can use your favorite dx cluster software or website to find us. For digital modes pskreporter.info and the like should have us listed. With HamAlert you can setup notifications for our call sign and know when and where we operate.
